About

Our project was created to provide consumers with on-demand movie and television content. Some individuals may not use subscription services enough to justify paying the recurring fee. Additionally, invididuals with low bandwidth may be unable to stream content effectively. Our project aims to give users options to physically rent, stream, or purchase to own. This flexibility which is not present on popular platforms can cater to individuals' movie and television watching preferences.
